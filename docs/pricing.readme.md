# FDAware Pricing Overview

FDAware offers simple, transparent pricing designed for FDA-regulated brands of all sizes. Choose the plan that fits your business needs and upgrade as you grow.

## Plans & Features

### Lite
- **Price:** $249/month
- **For:** Early-stage founders launching 1–2 products
- **SKU uploads per month:** 2
- **Features:**
  - Smart label upload (PDF, JPG, PNG)
  - AI-powered compliance analysis
  - Basic violation detection
  - Basic compliance reports (PDF only)
  - FDA regulation database access
  - 1 workspace, 1 user
  - Basic analytics dashboard
  - Email support

### Team
- **Price:** $499/month
- **For:** Growing brands shipping multiple SKUs
- **SKU uploads per month:** 10
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Lite
  - Up to 3 users, multi-brand workspaces
  - Enhanced PDF & CSV reports (with version history)
  - Full violation detection (all categories)
  - Trend analysis & compliance insights
  - Team collaboration (invite/manage team, role-based access)
  - Priority support
  - Full analytics dashboard

### Scale
- **Price:** $999/month
- **For:** Manufacturers, consultants & advanced users
- **SKU uploads per month:** 25
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Team
  - Up to 10 users, unlimited workspaces
  - Advanced reporting (bulk scan, custom filters)
  - Enterprise security (SOC2, RBAC, encryption)
  - Dedicated onboarding & support
